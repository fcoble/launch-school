Given a loan amount, APR, and loan durations in years, 
calculate the monthly interest rate and loan duration in months.

1.) Get input from the user
  a.) Loan amount - integer
  b.) APR (Annual Percentage Rate) - integer
  c.) Loan Duration in years - integer
2.) Calculate the monthly interest rate by dividing the APR by 12
3.) Calculate the loan duration in months by dividing the loan years by 12
3.) Use the monthly interest payment formula to calculate the monthly payment
4.) Output the monthly payment to the user formatted as dollars and cents
5.) If the user wishes to calculate different terms, restart the calculator, else quit
